Practical Introduction to Satellite Telemetry Data Reception

A workshop on "Practical Introduction to Satellite Telemetry Reception & Decoding" was conducted on 7th February 2019 at College of Engineering, Trivandrum by Dr.Raveendranath and Joji John Varghese. Activities includes brief introduction to theory, fabrication of VHF Quadrifilar Helix Antenna, RTL SDR Interfacing and Configuration.Workshop is concluded with live demonstration of NOAA Satellite APT Reception and Decoding.
